Music Therapy for Chronic Pain

Music therapy has been found to be an effective complementary treatment for chronic pain, with a number of benefits for individuals who suffer from this condition.

Benefits of music therapy for chronic pain

  • Reduced pain perception:
  • Reduced anxiety and depression
  • Improved mood and relaxation
  • Enhanced social interaction
